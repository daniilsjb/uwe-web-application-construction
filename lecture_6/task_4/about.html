About Riga

Riga is the capital and largest city of Latvia, home to over 600,000 inhabitants - about a third of Latvia's entire population!

Here are some facts about Riga:

  • The city name is pronounced like "Ree-gah".
  • Riga was founded in 1201. It is not so old!
  • In total, Riga occupies about 118 square miles.
  • Riga is located 29ft above sea level.
  • Riga is a member of the Union of the Baltic Cities, as well as the Union of Capitals of the European Union.
